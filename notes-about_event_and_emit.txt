What is an Event?
- An event is like a log statement that is recorded on the blockchain.
- Events help clients (like DApps or frontend applications) to listen for specific occurrences in a smart contract.
- They are primarily used to record important actions such as updates, transactions, or errors.

What is emit?
- The emit keyword is used to "fire" or trigger an event in Solidity.
- When an event is emitted, its data is stored in the blockchain transaction log.
